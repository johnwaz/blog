Greetings are in order

Hello, I am John Wasielewicz. It's not easy to admit to people you don't know, or people you do know for that matter, how being a former alcoholic for close to 20 years has drastically impacted your life in many ways. Currently I have been sober for over six years now and spent the last few years on the coding journey of becoming a developer. During that time frame I have been learning programming skills such as Java, Spring, Hibernate, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. I am presently on the track of learning React and will be sharing the lessons in this blog. Surely it will evolve into other things throughout the course of time.

If you're like me, blogging has not particularly been an interest except for running across them while looking up information. I have been advised to create a blog by someone who deceivingly seemed at first like a unique individual. It is very interesting to know that they have a company which is respected by software developers. This person can perfectly be described by what the Urban Dictionary has listed as one of the definitions of a "man thot". Typically I never write anything bad about people, but I would most likely not be doing this if it wasn't for them. I have to give credit for that alone.

So if you happen to be reading this one day, whoever you are, please do not judge me for any ignorance of not knowing or understanding things your particular way. I have been trying to the best of my ability to better myself in more ways than just programming.

They say, "Everything happens for a reason." I guess we'll all find out in due time.
